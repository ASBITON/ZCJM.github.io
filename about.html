About ArtCatalog

We curate and showcase artwork from emerging and established artists.

Design Principles

  • Contrast: Clear typographic and color contrast so artworks stand out.
  • Alignment: Grid layout for consistency across cards and images.
  • Repetition: Repeated UI patterns (cards, spacing) for predictability.
  • Proximity: Related items placed together (title + credit + CTA).
